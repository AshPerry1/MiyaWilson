About me

The person behind Move Well by Miya Wilson

I’m a full-time home flipper with a soft spot for forgotten floor plans and kitchens that deserve a second act. Move Well by Miya Wilson started as a promise to myself: every project should feel calm, honest, and a little bit brave—just like the families who’ll live there next.

When I’m not on a job site, I’m sketching tile layouts, hunting vintage hardware, or walking neighborhoods with my notebook and way too much coffee. I partner with trusted trades, keep communication tight, and treat your timeline like it’s my own.

Movement matters—whether it’s a wall that slides open or a first-time buyer finally getting keys.
